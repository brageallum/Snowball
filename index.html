Placeholder is a social network where people can feel comfortable no matter what issues they have. We match anyone who needs someone to talk to with either a volunteer or someone with similar interests. The volunteers will be vetted by our team to make sure they are trustworthy and respectful.

Facebook can be a scary place when you have something you're ashamed of. People with psychological problems are known to hide their problems online. We want to provide a platform where they can relax and be themselves, while benefitting from a supportive network to get through life's challenges. Current services that aim to provide that are lacking.

To motivate people to join as volunteers we will include a game-like feature where they can compete to be the highest rated by the people they help.
